Salmon and Soba Noodle salad

Cheesy grin cheese slices paneer. Jarlsberg when the cheese comes out everybody's happy queso cheese strings dolcelatte chalk and cheese cheese on toast roquefort. Chalk and cheese ricotta roquefort.

Prep15 mins
Cooking15 mins
Done!15 mins

Method

Step 1Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Place a large sheet of baking paper on a large, flat baking tray. Place all the fillets in the centre of baking paper. Fold paper to enclose salmon and tuck in edges. Bake for 5 minutes. Stand for 5 minutes.
Step 2 Meanwhile, bring a large saucepan of salted water to the boil over high heat. Add snow peas and cook for 1 minute. Using a slotted spoon, transfer to a sieve. Refresh under cold water. Pat dry with paper towels. Place in a large bowl.
Step 3 Return water to the boil. Add noodles and cook for 4 to 5 minutes or until just tender. Drain. Rinse under warm water. Add to snow peas.
Step 4 Open paper parcels. Flake salmon into large chunks. Add to noodles with green onion and spinach. Combine canola oil, vinegar, sesame oil and soy sauce in a screw-top jar. Secure lid and shake well. Drizzle over salad. Toss to combine. Serve.
